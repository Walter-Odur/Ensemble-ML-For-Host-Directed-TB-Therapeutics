Results: Dataset Overview and Quality Control

Figure 1. The dataset comprises 19,044 cells from TB-positive and TB-negative lung tissue.

Class imbalance: 88.6% TB-affected (n = 16,874) vs 11.4% Control (n = 2,170). HIV-only cells (n = 588) excluded.

QC metrics (genes/cell, UMI counts, mitochondrial fraction) showed comparable distributions between conditions, confirming biological rather than technical signal.
