Results: Dimensionality Reduction

 

Figure 2. PCA on 2,497 HVGs revealed partial but incomplete class separation.

PC1 and PC2 captured 8.5% and 5.3% of variance, respectively. Twenty PCs accounted for approximately 30% of total variance.

t-SNE embedding of a 5,000-cell subsample confirmed distinct clusters with partial overlap, indicating that high-dimensional modelling is required to fully resolve the transcriptomic distinction.
