Figure 5. Z-scored expression heatmap for the top 30 SHAP genes across 200 Control and 200 TB-affected cells.

Upregulated genes (TAOK1, S100A12, CCL18) showed elevated expression in TB-affected cells. Downregulated genes (RGS2, SPARC) were higher in controls.

Cell-to-cell variability within TB-affected cells reflects the heterogeneity of the granuloma microenvironment (Gideon et al., 2022).
