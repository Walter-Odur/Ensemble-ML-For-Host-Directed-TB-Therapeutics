Results: SHAP Disease Signature

 

Figure 4. SHAP analysis of LightGBM identified a 100-gene TB disease signature (59 up, 41 down).

Top genes: MTRNR2L12 (mitochondrial stress), TAOK1 (MAPK signalling), S100A12 (antimicrobial calgranulin), CCL18 (macrophage activation).

Downregulated: RGS2 (chemokine signalling), PLTP and APOC1 (lipid metabolism), reflecting metabolic reprogramming.
